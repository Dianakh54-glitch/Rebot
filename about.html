THE REBOTE STORY

Founded in 2024, Rebote was born to bridge the gap between high-performance sports gear and luxury fashion.

In Padel, the 'rebote' is the moment the ball hits the wall—a moment of tactical brilliance and resilience. Our handcrafted vegan leather bags are designed for the player who demands elegance both on and off the court.

Handcrafted. Elevated. Essential.
